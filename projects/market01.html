What is Market01?

We built Market01 to solve a key problem in the rapidly evolving AI and computing landscape: the lack of a unified, accessible, and intelligent platform for discovering, renting, and understanding GPUs. Existing tools are fragmented, with some focusing only on rentals, others on specs or availability. Market01 brings it all together.

What sets this launch apart is our end-to-end design philosophy. Market01 serves everyone, from beginners taking their first steps in GPU computing to AI frontier labs pushing the boundaries of large-scale research. Whether you’re a student experimenting with models, a startup scaling your AI stack, or a research lab needing reliable high-end GPU access and deep performance insights, Market01 meets you there.

We’re most proud of building a platform that’s both technically powerful and radically accessible—a place where GPU infrastructure becomes transparent, usable, and efficient for all. Market01 mainly focuses on:

  • Unified GPU Marketplace
  • Real-Time Availability & Pricing
  • Custom GPU Filters
  • GPU Intelligence Platform
  • Real time updates

Who are we?

We’re a team of two—a blend of engineers, designers, and AI researchers—driven by a shared mission to democratize access to GPU infrastructure. Frustrated by the complexity and fragmentation in the current GPU landscape, we set out to build something better: a platform that bridges the gap between hardware and humans.

Whether you’re just beginning your machine learning journey or operating at the cutting edge of AI research, we believe that accessing the right GPUs should be simple, fast, and transparent. Market01 is our answer to that vision—a modern GPU marketplace built for everyone, from curious learners to the frontier labs shaping tomorrow.
