Gray Swan Staged Attack Mar 2026

Gray Swan Arena ran a two wave challenge (11–25 March 2026): break layered AI defenses (model only, input filter, output filter, full pipeline, extended transcript), in the spirit of staged attacks on stacked safeguards. Judging involved UK AISI and US CAISI; sponsors included frontier labs.

I placed 29th on the overall leaderboard and received $100 from the overall prize pool.

Reinforcement learning

Policy gradients and variance Mar 2026

REINFORCE and actor–critic methods optimize expected return by pushing action probabilities in directions that improve rewards. Baselines and advantage estimates cut gradient variance so learning does not drown in noise.

Exploration versus exploitation Feb 2026

Bandits formalize the tradeoff: explore to discover better arms, exploit what already pays. In MDPs, epsilon–greedy, entropy bonuses, and count–based curiosity reshape how often the agent tries the unfamiliar.

Offline RL and shift Jan 2026

Policies trained only on logged behavior suffer when they visit states the dataset barely covers. Conservative objectives and uncertainty–aware critics aim to keep updates inside the data manifold.
